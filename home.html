About us

Art-hive was founded in 2021, and based in Nairobi, Kenya. Our intention is to create a strong and sustainable art market for Kenyan artists by supporting and promoting the most innovative and exciting artists. Through group and solo exhibitions and website to display their art, the gallery has increased visibility for established and emerging artists. we are building a strong and sustainable market for Kenyan artists. In response to the current global crisis, we are adapting our programming and enhancing our online platforms to continue promoting challenging and thought-provoking contemporary art from all regions in Kenya.

Gallery

Art-hive Gallery is open during the week from 10am to 5pm. One member of staff will be at the gallery every day to assist with viewing. We provide sanitiser, masks, gloves and a thermometer. We operate social distancing and only allow two visitors into the gallery at a time. You are all much welcome.Your amusement is our joy
