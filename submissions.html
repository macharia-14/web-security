Artists Submission

For all art submissions to be done here inorder to reduce copywriten material.

We request you to send art work to us the administrators to avoid copyright infringement, in that they can help you ensure that your artwork doesn't contain any copyrighted material or that it doesn't infringe on anyone else's intellectual property rights. By sending your art to us,we can review it and provide feedback to help you avoid potential legal issues. Additionally,we can advise you on how to properly attribute any existing works that may have inspired your own, and how to obtain permission to use any copyrighted material legally. This can save you time, money, and stress in the long run.
